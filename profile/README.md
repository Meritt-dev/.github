# Meritt Dev

We build, operate, and maintain production-ready software products and business platforms.

Meritt Dev is a UK software company behind practical SaaS products, internal tools, CRM workflows, e-commerce platforms, billing systems, and developer tools. We do not just ship code and disappear — we operate the systems we build, support users, maintain infrastructure, and live with the consequences of our technical decisions.

## What we build

We focus on software that businesses rely on every day:

- SaaS products
- CRM integrations and workflow automation
- Internal business tools
- Billing and operations platforms
- E-commerce and commerce systems
- API and backend platforms
- Developer productivity tools

## Products we operate

### LeadCall

Phone lead attribution and CRM automation for mobile sales teams.

LeadCall tracks inbound, outbound, and missed calls from existing business phones and syncs activity into CRM systems without call forwarding, tracking numbers, or VoIP migration.

### BillBridge

Billing and financial operations software for businesses that need reliable invoice, payment, and workflow management.

### RetroBridge

Collaborative team retrospectives for agile teams, with real-time boards, discussion, and team insights.

### Horus

AI-powered root cause analysis and production investigation platform for engineering teams.

## How we work

We are founder-led, engineering-first, and focused on long-term ownership.

When we build software, we also think about:

- How it will run in production
- How it will be maintained after launch
- How failures will be investigated
- How users will be supported
- How the system will evolve over time

We prefer practical engineering, boring technology where it makes sense, fewer moving parts, and systems a small senior team can actually operate.
